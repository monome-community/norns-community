# n16o

norns script, i2c-based ER-301 control via KORG nanoKONTROL2

### Requirements

- norns (191230 or later)
- crow (1.0.2 or later)
- ER-301 (tested with v0.4.27-stable, may work with v0.3-stable, but not tested)
- nanoKONTROL2
