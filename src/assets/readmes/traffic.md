# traffic

edit of @markeats' loom for quickly decoupling motifs from their harmonic framework

enc 1/arc 1: circle of 5ths

enc 2/arc 2: jump notes

enc 3: add/remove

key 2 (tap): major/minor

key 2 (hold) + enc 2:

select jump amount

key 3 (tap):

record pattern,

play pattern,

(hold) clear pattern
